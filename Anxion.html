ANXIETY

An intense or ongoing feeling of anxiety, fear, or uneasiness, sometimes without a known reason. Physical symptoms like restlessness, difficulty concentration, and a rapid pulse are possible.

  • Anxiety disorders are the most common mental disorders worldwide, affecting 301 million people in 2019(World Health Organization).
  • They occur in approximately 19.1% of U.S. adults annually, with lifetime prevalence reaching ~31% (National Institute of Mental Health).
  • Anxiety typically begins in childhood or adolescence, and women are more likely to be affected than men (World Health Organization).
  • Only about 25-43% of affected individuals receive treatment (World Health Organization).
